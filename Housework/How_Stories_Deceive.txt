====== How Stories Deceive ======
Created Freitag 13 Mai 2016
//Summary of How Stories Deceive by Maria Konnikova//
//http://www.newyorker.com/science/maria-konnikova/how-stories-deceive//

storytelling: an **emotional** way to make sense of the world & **bond** over shared knowledge, legends and history

==== Ways stories can deceive ====
* attention focused on in-the-moment emotional cues -> logical, analytic thinking get's overshadowed
* look at a situation from an emotional, empathetic viewpoint

==== Takeaways ====
* if you want to let your audience focus on the facts, don't overshadow them with emotional narration
* **empathy** is used in commercial narratives, etc. to **manipulate** people
