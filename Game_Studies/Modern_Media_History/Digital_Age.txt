===== Digital Age =====
Postmodernity to Present
-> digital work requires autonomous, creative, playful behaviour

**Defining Media: ** Digital Games
-> systematic, procedural, interactive, transmedial, global
-> fluid/non-linear

**Defining Class: **(Digital) Knowledge Workers
-> manipulate software and their virtual symbols

**Important Inventions:**
* software-based computer systems
* global networks/WWW

**Authorship/copyright:**
- see Industrial Age
- fall of the author, rise of the audience (creative participation, read-write culture)
